Voice 2020 is a televised SriLankan talent show competition , and is a part of global talent franchise created by Malindu Jetheka which is started in 2016. The program is produced by Stein Studio, distributed by the former, and broadcast on the GOLD television network,premiering on June 2009.The preliminary rounds took place at Sugathadasa Stadium Colombo, on October 30th, 31st and November 1st; at Royal College, Galle on November 08th and 10th and at Stein Studios on November 22th, 25th and 26th in 2019.Throughout the show,each participant who auditions attempts to secure a place in the live episodes of season by impressing panel of judges.Applicants were called and audition round held on 2020 Januuary and semi finals were chosen by the judge board.Public online voting round is available.Votes are valid only during the performance period .The page will be updated with new updates.Contestants perform and the first television premier started on 20th on May 2019 at 7.30pm.

Each year's competition begins with two rounds of auditions. The first round, referred to as "open auditions", are held across several different cities around the Sri Lanka during the Autumn months,within usual places that are attended by the producers.The second round, referred to as the "Judges' Auditions", are held the following year during January and February, within a select set of cities .The second round's name is due to the fact that these auditions are attended to by the judges, alongside an audience who secure tickets to attend these sessions.Every year, contestants of any age can audition for the televised contest .Once in the live rounds, in order to reach the final and a chance to win a cash prize, participants seek to impress the public and judges .
